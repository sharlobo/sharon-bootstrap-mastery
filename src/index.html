Welcome to Pirate Cove

A diary by Captain Barnaby Bubbles

Thar be monsters in the sea

Nakgharnak

Nakgharnak, a legendary sea creature, is said to dwell deep within the treacherous waters of Pirate Cove. With tentacles that reach out like shadowy appendages, Nakgharnak's mere presence strikes fear into the hearts of even the boldest pirates.

Gothnakgor

Gothnakgor is said to protect hidden treasures and ancient secrets buried within the depths of the cove. Its ominous presence serves as a constant reminder of the perils and wonders that await those who dare to venture into these waters.

Xisgharel

Xisgharel lures sailors into a world of enchantment and danger. Its siren-like call is said to lead ships to their doom or to hidden, untold treasures. Xisgharel remains an enigmatic figure, embodying the perilous allure of the sea.
